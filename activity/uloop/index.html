iwillseoulyou.kr

iwillseoulyou.kr looks like a guide application for tourists in Seoul. However, with its distracting interface, iwillseoulyou.kr introduces places in Seoul in a sarcastic way. Actually it is a website to satirize the social problems in Seoul. (This project was done by a team of 3 artists. I was in charge of development and design in this project. I also participated in planning this project.)
